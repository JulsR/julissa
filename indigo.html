An idea built as an space for art, Indigo represents a new community for artists.

The app will embrace the culture of those who desire to exhibit their personal journeys to friends, family and even to others around the world. Indigo app is a wonderful way to discover, explore, connect and share with others in the community the same passion for the visual medias. Ready to Live . Seek . Love The app will be best known for its journals entry, a feature that allow users to post a journal entry in order to keep track of their journeys and adventures. Indigo targets the young adults of Generation Z, who desires to connect and shared their experiences via their mobiles phones and smart devices (Android, iPhone, Ipad). Indigo App offers new and unique features, in order to distinguish ourself from our competitors.

Our Concept

Indigo is an interactive initiative that empowers users to create, discover, and connect.

The concept behind Indigo is to built a platform where users record and share their daily activities, events and memories through photos and videos. The app is a flexible photo community that provides its users a modern and user-friendly solution to upload their images, videos onto a minimalistic grid.

Brainstorming Time!

The fastest way to get something out of our heads is to grab a pen and a sheet of paper and quickly sketch it out.

Sketching, with an actual pen and paper, is a part to the design process that simply can only be mimicked with digital tools. They are rough or unfinished drawings or paintings, often made to assist in making a more finished picture.

Sketching is fast, rough, and dirty. Indigo sketches contain basic shapes like; circles, squares, rectangles, lines, arrows, and stick figures are enough to display the ideas of what is the visual style of the app.

Mobile user experience design is maturing. Sketching enables us to explore the problem space and define the solution space at the same time. It frames our current understanding of the problem.
