INDIGO

An idea built as an space for art,Indigo represents a new community for artists.

The app will embrace the culture of those who desire to exhibit their personal journeys to friends, family and even to others around the world. Indigo app is a wonderful way to discover, explore, connect and share with others in the community the same passion for the visual medias. Ready to Live . Seek . Love The app will be best known for its journals entry, a feature that allow users to post a journal entry in order to keep track of their journeys and adventures. Indigo targets the young adults of Generation Z, who desires to connect and shared their experiences via their mobiles phones and smart devices (Android, iPhone, Ipad). Indigo App offers new and unique features, in order to distinguish ourself from our competitors.

Indigo is an interactive initiative that empowers users to create, discover, and connect.

The concept behind Indigo is to built a platform where users record and share their daily activities, events and memories through photos and videos.
