CASE

COMAPA

Comapa has a wide experience in execution of works in civil engineering and mining, specialized in movement of ground, excavation, perforation and demolitions, production and handling of aggregation and asphalt.

They have a team of technical staff and qualified workers, with more than 20 years of experience in providing specialize services of engineering, such like:

  • Movement of ground and materials in general.
  • Excavations with backhoe (rock soil and underwater).
  • Production of Aggregate (aggregate mines on Andrés Boca Chica, La Romana,Hato Mayor and El Seibo).
  • Aggregate production for construction and production of coral stone.

Demolitions for civil jobs like highway construction, dam, marine, harbor, tunnels,among others.

The purpose of this project is to enter the 21st Century market by creating profitable marketing objectives that will accomplish the business goals. The goals are to promote the company services that will produce data worth and increase the company revenues. By designing an online platform with a modern look and feel the company will provide a new way of communication with their costumers. The intention is to make a user-friendly website, responsive and easier on the eyes

The idea for the online platform is todesign a modern, simple structure that will follow a grid which will fulfill the rows with different sections of the site on the homepage and the rest of the pages.

Challenge

Our vision was to design and developed a professional website with a user-friendly design. The objectives is to increase awareness to the brand's reputation and establish a strong online presence. Organization of content, eliminating clutter, incorporating social media, simplification of pages, and a reduction of colors are good practices of design for the web application.

Strategy Brief & Objectives

The strategy was to design a responsive website with a clean style which consisted of a well structural organization on the content. It included a detailed description of the company products and services along with images and videos. The goal was to use different types of skills – from graphic design, wireframing, prototyping to copywriting and programming - fused together in order to create an interface that will not only feature a pleasant aesthetic design but that will also communicate the mission and vision of the company.

Target Audience

Comapa targets a wide range of potential customers from a mature market of global leading companies to local small construction companies. Customers within the market consist of commercial construction companies, equipment rental and leasing companies, general construction firms.

Clients Needs

Comapa has a great deal of experience in projects of earth movement. In the past five years they have carried out under contract, important projects regarding excavation and other developments. The company needs an online platform to display the corporation’s portfolio.

Goal

Design a simple and unique professional look that will attract potential clients. Increase the amount of potential clients as well as allow a penetration into markets outside the Dominican Republic. At the moment the company does not have an existing site or any graphic presence on the web.
