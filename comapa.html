CASE

Comapa

Comapa has a wide experience in execution of works in civil engineering and mining, specialized in movement of ground, excavation, perforation and demolitions, production and handling of aggregation and asphalt.

They have a team of technical staff and qualified workers, with more than 20 years of experience in providing specialize services of engineering, such like:

  • Movement of ground and materials in general.
  • Aggregate production for construction and production of coral stone.

Demolitions for civil jobs like highway construction, dam, marine, harbor, tunnels,among others.

The purpose of this project is to enter the 21st Century market by creating profitable marketing objectives that will accomplish the business goals. The goals are to promote the company services that will produce data worth and increase the company revenues. By designing an online platform with a modern look and feel the company will provide a new way of communication with their costumers. The intention is to make a user-friendly website, responsive and easier on the eyes
