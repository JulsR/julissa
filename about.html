USER EXPERIENCE

"I can always find love, but eventually I get horny for my creativity." - Gilda Radner


My hands-on expertise is on UX design which allows me to create concepts and interactive initiatives. The challenge of translating a products vision and business goals into compelling user experience has giving me the security to build high-level design strategies which I greatly enjoy and do my best to see the concept prevailed. Personally I'm drawn by the innovative solution and take great pleasure in using my creativity, logic and design skills while developing cross-platform design services and applications. My strengths are in design, development and understanding of the user experience.

Out of my passion for design I'm driven and strive for excellence when creating a user-centered design experience. After working as a fashion stylist for a few years, I have decided to get an BFA in Web/Interactive Media at Ai Miami International University of Art & Design. I realized that although fashion was one of my passions I felt more compelled and attracted with the future of Web development, Interactive design and it's cross-disciplinary activities.
